Terms of Service
01.

Discord ToS

All members must adhere to the official Discord Terms of Service and Community Guidelines at all times.

02.

Respect & Conduct

Harassment, hate speech, or toxicity towards any member of Cavern will result in an immediate permanent ban.

03.

No Self-Promotion

Advertising other Discord servers, social media, or external services without explicit permission is forbidden.

04.

Malicious Content

Sharing malware, phishing links, or attempting to compromise the security of other users is strictly prohibited.

05.

NSFW Policy

Cavern is a SFW environment. Posting sexually explicit or gore-related content is not permitted in any channel.

06.

Staff Authority

Our moderation team has the final word on all disputes. Evading a punishment will result in a network-wide blacklist.

07.

Spam & Flooding

Do not spam messages, emojis, or mass-mention users. Keep bot commands in their designated channels.

08.

Account Integrity

You are responsible for your account. "My friend used my computer" is not a valid excuse for rule violations.

Privacy Policy
01.

Data Collection

We log user IDs and message metadata to prevent raids and ensure the safety of the Cavern community.

02.

Third-Party Apps

Our custom bots process basic profile information to manage roles, levels, and server security features.

03.

Information Sharing

Cavern never sells user data. Data is only shared with law enforcement if required by a legal warrant.

04.

Voice Privacy

Voice conversations are not recorded by the server staff, though users should remain aware of Discord's own logging.

05.

Data Retention

Verification data and moderation logs are kept for a period of 6 months before being automatically purged.

06.

User Rights

You may request a copy of your stored moderation history or request a full data wipe upon leaving the server.

07.

Cookie Policy

This web landing page uses only essential security cookies to prevent cross-site request forgery attacks.

08.

Staff Access

Only authorized administrators have access to back-end logs. All access is audited to prevent abuse.
